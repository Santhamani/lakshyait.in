About Us

Lakshya IT Consulting Private Limited has started its journey in 2014 as an IT Service Provider to meet the Talent needs of its clients. With a mere start with 5 employees and 4 clients now Lakshya IT stands in the domestic and international market with over 50+ employees as our fulltime in house stakeholders and over 240+ and counting employees at various client locations on our roles.

Lakshya IT has built a valuable space among the customers heart over the time. We are proud to say that “Lakshya IT is a customer oriented company”. Lakshya invests time in two ways one in recruiting a right talent and second to execute the product.

Lakshya IT Services which are more Customer Centric includes Application Development & Support, Maintenance, Application Testing, Designing & Implementation and Talent Acquisition.

Our Vision

Lakshya IT always aims to deliver Products and Services in-time with higher standards, which add value to our customers business and brings a positive impact which adds longevity on their business needs.

Our Mission
